About Me

I'm Connor J. Link, an aspiring hardware engineer and a hobbyist programmer. A spring 2023 graduate of Wahlert Catholic High School, I plan to pursue further studies in technology as a Computer Engineering major starting at Iowa State University beginning in the fall of 2023.

In my free time, I enjoy programming, practicing piano, shooting and editing photos and videos, working out, and playing puzzle video games. Filled with curiosity and a passion to learn, I touch on every topic I can in my research—space, aviation, automobiles, and computing among many.

Through my past work experience, volunteering, and my participation in FIRST Tech Challenge as my team's lead developer, I have honed a number of industry-focused skills. These include collaboration, project management, leadership, critical thinking, and problem solving to name some. These are, naturally, aside from the other skills, like programming, CAD modeling, Arduino development, and version control with Git and GitHub that I have learned both independently and through the aforementioned experiences.
