Introduction

I'm Connor J. Link, an aspiring software and hardware engineer and avid hobbyist programmer. I began my further studies in technology as a Computer Engineering major at Iowa State University in the fall of 2023, and I look forward to studying abroad in Ireland during the autumn 2024 semester. Then, I plan to return to ISU to complete my Bachelor's of Science to graduate in the spring of 2026.

In my free time, I enjoy programming, practicing piano, shooting and editing photos and videos, and playing puzzle-based video games. I love spending time outdoors, too, where I concentrate on endurance sports like running and cycling as a fitness enthusiast. Filled with unending curiosity and a passion to learn, I touch on every topic I can in my personal research—space, aviation, automobiles, and computing among many.

Through my research and work experience, volunteering, and my participation in FIRST Tech Challenge as my team's lead developer, I have honed a number of industry-focused skills. These include collaboration, project management, leadership, critical thinking, and problem solving to name some. These are in addition to other skills, like programming, CAD modeling, Arduino development, and version control with Git and GitHub that I have learned both independently and through the aforementioned experiences.
